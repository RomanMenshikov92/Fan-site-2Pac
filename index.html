New Album: Outlawz - One Nation

In 1996, 2Pac announced the "One Nation" project, in which he wanted to unite the performers of the west and east coasts in order to stop the stupid feud. Unfortunately, he did not have time to carry out his plans because of his own death. After 25 years, the Outlawz band decided to bring the idea of their leader to life by recording the album "One Nation". Artists whose participation was supposed by Tupac himself were invited to work on it, among others. Among them are members of Boot Camp Click (Rockness Monsta, Smif-n-wessun, Buckshot), Krayzie Bone and DJ Premier.

BMW in which rapper 2pac was killed sells for $ 1,500,000

In Las Vegas, in one of the car dealerships, a 1996 BMW 7 series was put up for sale in which the famous rapper Tupac Shakur was shot.
